    our approach

    In our societies today, there are a lot of challenges that need US, that need solutions, solutions that can be long-lasting, sustainable, life-changing, and refreshing to our societies. Among them is the challenge of marginalisation and inequitable sharing and distribution of county and national resources. This brings with it a lot of disparities such as high poverty levels, lack of proper access to quality education hence higher dependency ratios. That's why, Zoghori Sacco through Zoghori Foundation and its partners - The Zoghori Champions of Hope, has resolved to help in building and transforming society through the mobilisation of both financial and human resources. Ours is a calling anchored on the foundations of Building & Transformation of people's lives through activities such as offering sustainable education scholarships, mentorship programs, and counseling services to our youths and supporting environmental conservation initiatives. We believe in the Building & Transformation of one another through concerted and unified efforts, planning, and prudential management of acquired resources to achieve our mantra of TRANSFORM A LIFE TO CHANGE THE WORLD

    WHAT WE ARE DOING...

    Zoghori Foundation is the vehicle for Zoghori Sacco Society Ltd for Corporate Social Responsibility. The current flagship project is an EDUCATION SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME for bright but less fortunate children who are facing difficulties incpaying school fees for both secondary and university education. Zoghori Foundation was established in 2019 as a registered trust in recognition of the need to enhance the existing Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives by the society. At that point, the society felt it was its duty to not only grow financially but also develop a deeper commitment to help enhance the growth of the community. We believe that our role goes beyond profit-making to include promoting long-term economic prosperity and quality of life for everyone in our communities. We intend to give hope to every achievable dream and give life to the hopeless. Join us in this journey of serving humanity through the basic acts of transformation and building one another!

    Youth Counselling Services

    Counselling is the most common form of talking therapy. It can help young people deal with issues and events and the effects they are having on their mental well-being. Counselling could be recommended for young people who are basically healthy but who are struggling with a mental health disorder such as depression or eating disorders; it can address problems with anxiety, bereavement; bullying, anger, relationships, low self-esteem, and self-harm. The counsellor will help explore the problem, the symptoms and strategies for coping.

    QUALIFYING FOR THE SCHOLARSHIPS

    For order, openness, efficiency and accountability, the foundation through its able leadership, has set aside a few parameters for any individual to be accepted for sponsorship through Zoghori Foundation.

    Be able to verify the position of need.

    ZOGHORI CHAMPION OF HOPE

    Zoghori Champion of Hope

    This is an individual or institution willing to commit a certain amount of money, either given monthly, quarterly, or annually to the Zoghori Foundation to support its activities
    in ensuring a needy situation will have a solution.

    Our belief is that there is hope in every young person to succeed in life no matter his or her background,
    hence our responsibility to partner with Zoghori Champions of Hope to fulfil their dreams.

    Qualifying for Zoghori Champion of Hope

    By either the individual or institution making a commitment to commit a monthly donation of kshs.50 to Kshs.1,000 and above, paid either monthly, quarterly, half-yearly or yearly.

    By seeking a donation from kshs.50 is that no one with a heart to touch a needy situation is locked out.

    WHAT WE ARE DOING...

    Zoghori Foundation is the SOCIAL ARM of Zoghori Sacco for Corporate Social Responsibility established in 2019 as a registered trust in recognition of the need to enhance the existing Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives by society. At that point, the Sacco felt it was its duty to not only grow financially but also develop a deeper commitment to help enhance the growth of the community. The current Zoghori Foundation's flagship project is an Education SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME for bright but less fortunate children who are facing difficulties in paying school fees for both secondary and university education. In the year 2016, Zoghori Sacco Society Limited successfully rolled out an Education program to pay school fees for needy secondary school students as an integral part of its CSR projects. The program will continue in the days ahead, to enable these children to achieve their full potential. We believe that our role goes beyond profit-making to include promoting long-term economic prosperity and quality of life for everyone in our communities. If our people prosper, we prosper as a society and soar to unimaginable heights of achieving our motto of BUILDING ONE ANOTHER.
